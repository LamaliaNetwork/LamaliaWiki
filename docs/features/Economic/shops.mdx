## Global Market

Rotating Shop is a special shop with a limited quantity that resets every 1 hour after the limited quantity runs out.
This shop rotates every 6 hours and displays all items available in server shop.

:::caution
Hourly Sales Limit (Refresh every 1 hour after the limit is reached) Rotate every 6 hours as usual 2 AM, 8 AM, 2 PM, 8 AM
:::

  </TabItem>

## Head Shop

The Head Shop is managed by the NPC of the same name. Acquire unique Heads for personal use or decoration.

:::tip Access Requirements
- Required at Rank **Diamond** rank to access.
:::

  </TabItem>

## Item Buyer

Simplify your selling process with the **Item Buyer** NPC. Quick-sell your items directly to the Local Market.

:::caution
Quick sell is not linked to the Rotation Shop.
:::

  </TabItem>

## Player Shop

Participate in the dynamic **Player Shop**. Set your prices, sell your items, and enjoy a tax-free transaction environment, benefiting both buyers and sellers.

:::tip Supporter+ Perk
If you're **Supporter+**, you can use `/ah` to access the Player Shop from anywhere.
:::
